Dr. Shabnam Sodagari is an associate professor of Computer Engineering and Computer Science (CECS). She received her PhD degree from Penn State (Pennsylvania State University). Her current research interests include quantum computing, applications of data science, AI and machine learning in various domains, such as health, education, etc. Her expertise further includes providing consultation to Academic Affairs in support of the CSU Chancellor's Office initiatives. She is a senior member of the IEEE and has several publications at impactful journals and flagship conferences.
